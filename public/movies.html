My list of Best Movies

  1. Pursuit of happyness

  2. Shawshank Redemption

  3. Cast away

Pursuit of happyness

  • Based on a true story about a man named Christopher Gardner.

  • A struggling salesman takes custody of his son as he's poised to begin a life-changing professional career.

Shawshank Redemption

  • The Shawshank Redemption has become a classic film - it's even IMDb's top-rated movie of all time.

  • Over the course of several years, two convicts form a friendship, seeking consolation and, eventually, redemption through basic compassion.

Cast away

  • FedEx systems engineer Chuck Noland has his ruled-by-the-clock existence abruptly end when a harrowing plane crash leaves him isolated on a remote island. As Chuck struggles to survive, he finds that his own personal journey has only just begun.

  • Nominated for 2 Oscars. 15 wins & 38 nominations total.
